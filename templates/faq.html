Jewelry Care

To maintain the brilliance of your pieces:

  • Avoid contact with water, perfumes, lotions, and hairspray.
  • Remove jewelry before bathing, swimming, or exercising.
  • Store each piece separately in the original DADSON box or a soft pouch to prevent scratching.
  • Gently wipe with a soft, dry cloth after wearing.

Store your DADSON pieces in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Use individual pouches or compartments to prevent pieces from tangling or scratching each other. Airtight zip-lock bags can also help slow down tarnishing.

For light dulling, gently buff the piece with a soft microfiber cloth. Avoid using chemical cleaners or abrasive materials. For significant tarnish, visit a local jeweller for professional polishing. Note that over-polishing can wear down the plating over time.

With proper care, our anti-tarnish plating can last 1–3 years with daily wear. The longevity depends on your skin type, lifestyle, and how well you follow care instructions. Avoiding moisture and chemicals significantly extends the life of your piece.
